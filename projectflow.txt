1. create a virtual environment
2. install necessary libraries and packages

3. make sure to have an account on huggingface
4. create an access token - Token type: Read (This token has read-only access to all your and your orgs resources and 
                                                    can make calls to Inference Providers on your behalf.)

5. In your terminal type(make sure to have "transformers" loaded in requirements only then this "hf-cli login" works.):

        huggingface-cli login > press enter > press Right click to paste copied token > press enter

6. write your code, specify your model to use such as (model="facebook/bart-large-cnn")
7. let the model download in our venv and the task to be accomplished.
